Long term goal

My long term goal is to be location independent. Since I love travel so much, I'd like to be a digital nomad doing things I love and passionate about.

My strenght is my passion for learning new things. I am interested in a wide variety of subjects. I always feel there are so much to learn with so little time. On the flip side however, I may not go deep with each of these subjects. Another one of my limitations is that I don't like to ask for help. I tend to solve my own problems via Google or Youtube as I'd like to be self-sufficient. But I realize it's important to ask others for help sometimes, not only to solve problems, but also to build meaningful relationships.

The non-tech skill I would like to develop at Dev Academy is effective communication in a virtual environment. I prefer face-to-face communication over online whenever possible. But the world we live in today has changed drastically comparing to just a couple of years ago. Most of the activites have been moved online. Therefore, I'd like to get better at communicating in the cyberspace.

My commitment

I have been mentally preparing myself for this course for some time now and told family and friends that I need to be laser-foucsed for the upcoming bootcamp. They are all very supportive. It is not only my commitment to bootcamp, but also a commitment to myself.

Self-care

In general, I am a very laid-back person. I don't get stressed easily (except when I have a deadline that I can't meet). I believe that practicing mindfulness plays a big role in that. Meditation and journalling helps to ground me which clears my mind. Daily movement is extremely important to me. Be it going to the gym or going out for a long walk by the water. A change of scenery will often provide me with a fresh new perspective on the problems I am trying to solve.
